WE ARE THE BEST

We represent an elite roster of talent across the Premier League, Bundesliga and major global leagues

Unique Sports Group maintains a position at the pinnacle of the sports representation industry. Born out of the shared vision of Unique Sports Management (USM) and International Sports Management (ISMG), the joining of agencies enables us to provide our clients with unrivalled knowledge and expertise. Working with the same drive, dedication and ambition as our clients, the Unique team ethos ensures that the success on the pitch is matched in the negotiations and career opportunities delivered beyond it. From academy to full international to plans for retirement, Unique has an established track-record in guiding, nurturing and assisting players at every stage to achieve their career goals.
